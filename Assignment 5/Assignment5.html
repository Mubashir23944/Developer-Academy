Comparison Opertators

Comparision operators are used in programming to compare two variables. The commom type of operators are equal to '==', less than'<', greater than '>' , less than equal to'<=', greater than equal to'>='.
The examples are shown in console log
Equal to (==): This operator is used to match if two variables are equal to one another.
Less than (<): This operator is used to match if the left variable is less than the variable on the right.
Greater than (>): This operator is used to match if the left variable is greater than the variable on the right.
Less than equal to (<=): This operator is used to match if the left variable is less than and equal to the variable on the right.
Greater than equal to (<=): This operator is used to match if the left variable is greater than and equal to the variable on the right.
